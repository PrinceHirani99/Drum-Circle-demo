A drum circle is a group music-making activity where participants sit in a circle and play various percussion instruments. No prior musical experience is needed. Our facilitator guides the session, teaching basic rhythms and building energy through collaborative music-making.
Our drum circles can accommodate anywhere from 10 to 1000 participants. For corporate team building, we typically work with groups of 20-100 people. For large events like weddings or festivals, we can handle much larger crowds with multiple facilitators.
We provide all necessary instruments including djembes, bongos, shakers, tambourines, and other percussion instruments. All instruments are sanitized and maintained to the highest standards. Participants don't need to bring anything.
Sessions typically run 45-90 minutes depending on your needs. Corporate team building sessions are usually 60 minutes, while wedding celebrations can be 45-75 minutes. We can customize the duration based on your event schedule.
Absolutely not! Drum circles are designed for everyone, regardless of musical background. Our expert facilitation ensures that even complete beginners feel comfortable and engaged within minutes.
Yes, we conduct drum circles in both indoor and outdoor settings. For outdoor events, we have weather-contingency plans and can provide covered setups if needed. Indoor venues should have adequate space for participants to sit in a circle.
Drum circles break down hierarchies, improve communication, build trust, enhance creativity, reduce stress, and create shared positive experiences. Teams report improved collaboration and stronger relationships after our sessions.
We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak event seasons (October-March for weddings, year-round for corporate events). However, we can often accommodate last-minute requests based on availability.
